The Mycelial Sourcelink generates a moderate amount of Source from nearby food, also being able to pull items from adjacent pedestals. Source will be output from the Sourcelink to nearby Source Jars within 5 blocks.

The amount of Source generated varies per food item, and is dependent on how nourishing it is. Source Berry foods are worth far more than other mundane foods. 

| Max Source | Transfer Rate | Event Range (Blocks) | Event Interval (Seconds) |
| ---------- | ------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------ |
| 20000      | 10000         | 1                    | 2                        |

## Source Per Item

| Item Type        | Source Generated                       | Progress |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Base             | `(11 * nutrition) + (60 * saturation)` | 1        |
| Magic Plants[^1] | `(Base + 10) * 2.5`                    | 5        |
| Magic Food[^2]   | `(Base + 10) * 2.5`                    | 5        |

## Block Conversion

Additionally, when the total progress of the Mycelial Sourcelink reaches certain values, Grass or Dirt in the 3x3 below it is converted into Mycelium and will grow mushrooms around it given that the space is empty. 

| From                 | To               | Progress Used | Range (Centered) |
| -------------------- | ---------------- | ------------- | ---------------- |
| Dirt Block           | Mycelium         | 25            | 3x1x3            |
| Grass Block          | Mycelium         | 25            | 3x1x3            |
| Air[^3]              | Brown Mushroom   | 10            | 3x3x3            |
| Air[^3]              | Red Mushroom     | 10            | 3x3x3            |


[^1]: <span class="badge text-bg-info">Item Tag</span> <span class="badge text-bg-dark">ars_nouveau:magic_food</span>
[^2]: <span class="badge text-bg-info">Block Tag</span> <span class="badge text-bg-dark">ars_nouveau:magic_plants</span>
[^3]: Must be above Mycelium.

The Agronomic Sourcelink generates Source from crop and tree growth within 15 blocks. Source will be output from the Sourcelink to nearby Source Jars within 5 blocks. 

Bonus Source is generated for magical plants such as Mageblooms, Source Berry Bushes, and Archwood Saplings. Note however, that bonemealing crops will not grant Source. 

Due to their low productivity, it is generally not advisable to use Agronomic Sourcelinks as your main method of Source production. It is most commonly used to sustainably power a Harvest Ritual while also generating a small surplus.

| Max Source | Transfer Rate | Event Range (Blocks) |
|------------|---------------|----------------------|
| 20000      | 10000         | 15                   |

| Non-Magical Tree | 50               |
